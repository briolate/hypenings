FIND AND BE FOUND


HYPEnings allows users to create digital flyers to a one mile radius of wherever they're posted. Other users can then find these flyers with the app upon entering its radius.

EXPLORE YOUR CITY


This symbiotic app encourages both local vendors to digitally promote fleeting deals wherever they want and users to find them by exploring their surroundings. But you gotta move! Flyers disappear 48 hours after posting.
